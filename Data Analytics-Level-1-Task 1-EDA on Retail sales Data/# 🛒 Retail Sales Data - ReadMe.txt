# 🛒 Retail Sales Data - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Retail Sales dataset using Python. The objective is to understand customer purchasing behavior, analyze sales trends, identify high-performing product categories, and generate actionable business insights through statistical analysis and data visualization.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
- Analyze customer demographics.
- Study monthly and quarterly sales trends.
- Identify product category performance.
- Visualize relationships among variables.
- Generate business recommendations based on insights.

---

## 🗂️ Dataset Information

- **Dataset Name:** Retail Sales Dataset
- **Total Records:** 1000
- **Total Features:** 18 (Cleaned dataset used for analysis)
- **Source:** Kaggle

### Features Used

- Transaction ID
- Date
- Gender
- Age
- Age Group
- Product Category
- Quantity
- Price per Unit
- Total Amount
- Month
- Quarter

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Sales vary across different months and quarters.
- Adults represent the largest customer segment.
- Product categories contribute differently to total revenue.
- Quantity and Price per Unit strongly influence Total Amount.
- Customer purchasing patterns help identify business opportunities.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Launch targeted marketing campaigns for key customer age groups.
- Offer promotional discounts during low-sales periods.
- Optimize pricing strategies based on customer purchasing behavior.
- Monitor monthly and quarterly sales trends for better planning.
